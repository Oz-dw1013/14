About Us

Every human begins their learning process the day they are born. Humans learn things every single day, no matter how old a person is, they will still continue to learn. There are many different techniques of learning. Everyone has their own way of comprehending certain skills. Humans begin to adapt certain ways of learning when they are little and they continue to use it for the rest of their lives. These techniques we use to learn come from a guy by the name of Howard Gardener. Gardener states that there are nine everyday intelligences that people use to learn. Every human uses at least one of the techniques to learn. People do not think about it when they use it, because it just happens naturally..
